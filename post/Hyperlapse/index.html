Hyperlapse on FFM

September 28, 2015

Approaching FFM from gloob on Vimeo.

Video shooted with Hyperlapse of instagram in an Airbus a319 approaching Frankfurt in a perfect sunny day. Music from Led Zeppelin :-)

From last summer I start to be interested on the technology behind hyperlapse.

Basically is a technology that take advantage of todays algorithm to stabilize the image, accelerate the action and render the missing parts with information of previous frames. I think that this paper from Microsoft Research is the most interesting to start to dig on this.

I used the iOS application Hyperlapse of Instagram that use the spatial sensors from the mobile to build the camera path.

A worth to view it.
